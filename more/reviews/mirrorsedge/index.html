portal - game review

general info

developer: dice year of release: 2009 genre: platformer platform played on: ps3 should you play it: yes! mirror's edge is a 3d platformer higly focused on movement and parkour in higly crammed and urban landscapes. the playtime is about 3-4 hours, but with a high replayability. it is structured in 11 levels: a tutorial, which somehow still has a lot of things to do, a prologue, and 9 chapters.

thoughts

it is an amazing game my only criticisms are: 1) the performance: on the ps3, at least, every time more than one portal is rendered there is a massive lag spike; 2) the last escape sequence along with the boss battle are a little dull, and definitely not as fast paced and exciting as the game would want to make you think. 3) it's a little sad that the game has no more content after the bossfigh: although i don't like never ending games, a couple extra levels or just being able to replay any without having to start over would be nice.
