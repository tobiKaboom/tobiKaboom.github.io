DS

The DS wasn't a console I used as a kid, but in 2024 I picked up my uncle's old one. It had a cracked top display and broken digitized, which I quickly fixed. But... then another problem arose. I did not have the charger for it (how do you even forget that T-T). So, after consulting documentation, trying to search for someone who already did something like this, etc... I got an USB-A to USB-C adapter and started working on it. I had to trim both the DS shell and the USB-A to C adapter (which I used to get more space to solder the power wires to), with help from the master dremeler, my father.
