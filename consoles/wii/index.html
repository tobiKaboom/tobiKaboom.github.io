Wii

I have used 2 Wiis, both second revisions. One was my brother's childhood Wii, which I modded in 2020, during the Covid lockdown, and the second is a Wii I bought later on specifically to hopefully optimize it for my use case. I started out by following the great guide on wii.hacks.guide and installed the Homebrew Channel, along with the proper brick protection. Then, I made a NAND backup and installed a couple Homebrew apps.
